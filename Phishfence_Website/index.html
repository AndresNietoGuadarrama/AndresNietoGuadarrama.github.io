Problem Statement

Domain Validated (DV) certificates represent 94% of the entire market due to the ease and speed with which they can be issued by the certificate authorities (CAs). Despite its popularity, CAsâ€™ flimsy validation filters for DVs opens the door for misuse. This problem is costly: According to the 2024 FBI Crime Report survey, a total loss of $12.5B could be attributable to cybercrimes. Thus, the aim of this project is to provide a data-driven, automated tool that aids CAs validation teams on detecting potentially malicious domains before the DV certificate is issued.
